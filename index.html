Our Schools

DAV Public School had a noble beginning in the year 1987 in a rented building with hardly 120 students. Today its multi-storied beautiful infra structure facilitating education to more than 2000 students speaks volume of its popularity and efficacy. The school is managed by DAV College Managing Committee, New Delhi and got affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education in the year 1995. Right from the day of its existence DAV is working persistently to shape the future of young and vibrant souls by imparting quality education with a team of 76 dedicated and smart working teachers. At present, the school stands out an enviable position at state level as many of the laudable achievements are to its credit. Class X and XII CBSE Board Exams results are par excellence.

About DAV

DAV Public School had a noble beginning in the year 1987 in a rented building with hardly 120 students. Today its multi-storied beautiful infra structure facilitating education to more than 2000 students speaks volume of its popularity and efficacy. The school is managed by DAV College Managing Committee, New Delhi and got affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education in the year 1995. Right from the day of its existence DAV is working persistently to shape the future of young and vibrant souls by imparting quality education with a team of 76 dedicated and smart working teachers. At present, the school stands out an enviable position at state level as many of the laudable achievements are to its credit. Class X and XII CBSE Board Exams results are par excellence. Adding value to CBSE’s inherent strength school is empowering thousands of its pupils by giving them a solid backing under the guidance of DAV Stalwarts. With a mission to provide a wide range of holistic education based on “skill and application”, we are working well to meet soaring expectations.
